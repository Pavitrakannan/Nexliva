Who We Are

Designing Homes,
Crafting Memories

Nexliva Interior was born from a simple belief — your home should feel like you. Founded by Ms. Pari Bhadane and Mst. Shree, we bring together deep design expertise, meticulous craftsmanship, and a genuine love for transforming spaces.

Based at Sinhgad Rd, Nanded, Pune, our team specialises in creating stylish, functional, and sustainable home interiors for apartments, villas, and commercial spaces. We take care of every detail — from the first sketch to the final nail.

How It Works

Our Design Process

01

Free Consultation

We start with a conversation — understanding your vision, lifestyle, and budget. No jargon, just a genuine discussion about your dream space.

02

Design Concept

Our designers craft mood boards, 2D floor plans, and 3D renders so you can visualise exactly how your space will look.

03

Material Selection

We guide you through material choices — from laminates and tiles to fabrics and fixtures — within your budget.

04

Execution & Build

Our on-site team brings the designs to life with precision. You receive regular updates throughout the build phase.

05

Handover & Styling

We do a thorough walk-through, handle all finishing touches, and style the space before handing over your dream home.
